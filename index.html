About Us

Our Story

I remember how our Lola Nalieta would sit outside every morning with a strong cup of barako coffee and warm pandesal. Freshly brewed in a cast iron pot, you could smell the coffee from the other side of the road.

The Owner

This is Nathalie, she likes to draw beautiful things and faces of people, whenever she smells coffee she would turned lively and alert. She would like to make her coffee business grow as she learned many techniques of coffee business.

Our Mission

To provide perfect, delicious coffee products and excellent, reliable services to our customers at reasonable prices; To earn the trust of our customers, employees, partners and our community and to contribute to good development and prosperity.
